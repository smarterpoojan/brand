Business Setup — Kaun sa Structure Chune?
💡 ₹30K Budget ke liye Recommendation
Tumhara budget limited hai, isliye Sole Proprietorship sab se best option hai. Koi registration fee nahi, GST se hi kaam chalega. Jab business grow kare (6-12 months baad), tab Private Limited mein convert kar sakte ho.

Private Limited ₹8,000–15,000 Complex 25% corporate Limited Growth stage / Investment

Sole Proprietorship — Kaise banate hain?

Technically koi separate registration nahi hoti. Bas GST registration lo + current account kholo apne business naam se. Business naam decide karo (e.g., "Dropzone Apparel" ya "Oversized Co.").

✅ Sole Proprietorship ke Fayde (Tum jaise Beginner ke liye)
  • Registration fee zero — same din shuru karo
  • Compliance bahut simple — ek GST return file karna hoga
  • Bank account asaani se khulta hai
  • Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra — teeno accept karte hain
  • Agar business nahi chala, band karna bhi simple

GST Registration — Step by Step

1

GST Portal par jao: gst.gov.in

"New Registration" click karo → "Taxpayer" select karo

2

Part A Fill karo

Legal name (PAN se match karna chahiye), email, mobile number → OTP verify karo

3

Part B Fill karo

Business details: address (Motera, Ahmedabad), business type, HSN code for T-shirts = 6109 (T-shirts, singlets, other vests, knitted)

4

Documents Upload karo

PAN, Aadhar, Address proof (electricity bill), bank statement/cancelled cheque, photo

5

ARN Number milega

Application Reference Number — isse track kar sakte ho. 3–7 working days mein GSTIN milega.

📌 GST Tax Rate for T-Shirts (2025)
  • T-shirts priced up to ₹999 → GST = 5% (HSN 6109)
  • T-shirts priced ₹1,000+ → GST = 12%
  • Tumhara selling price ₹600–900 rakho → 5% GST zone mein rehna affordable hai

GS1 Barcode — Beginner ke liye Shortcut

⚡ Budget-saving tip
  • Amazon ke liye: Amazon ka apna ASIN hota hai — ek baar listing banao, barcode ki zaroorat shuruat mein kam hoti hai. Amazon Brand Registry ke liye brand naam chahiye.
  • GS1 India: gs1india.org — basic annual membership ₹5,000 se shuru. 10 barcodes milte hain.
  • Free alternative: Flipkart apna FSN deta hai, Myntra apna Style ID. Shuruat mein GS1 delay kar sakte ho (2-3 months).

Trademark — Zarurat Hai Ya Nahi?

Kya hota hai: Tumhara brand naam (logo ke saath) legally protect hota hai. Koi copy nahi kar sakta.

Process: ipindia.gov.in → Trademark e-filing → Class 25 (Clothing)

Cost (small entity): ₹4,500 per class online

Timeline: 18–36 months (filing ke baad "TM" use kar sakte ho)

Renewal: Har 10 saal mein ₹9,000

₹30K Budget Advice
  • Phase 1 (Month 1-3): Trademark mat karo — save karo
  • Phase 2 (Month 4-6): Jab brand naam finalize ho, tab file karo
  • Filing ke turant baad "TM" symbol use kar sakte ho
  • Brand Registry ke liye Amazon pe trademark zaruri hai

Packaging Recommendations

📦 ₹30K Budget Packaging Strategy
  • Poly Bag: Plain white/black ziplock bags (₹3–5 per piece) — 100 pcs = ₹300–500
  • Thank You Card: Canva se design karo, local printer se print karo (₹0.50 per card) — 100 pcs = ₹50
  • Sticker (Brand Name): Round sticker logo seal (₹1–2 per piece)
  • Hang Tag: 300 gsm card print karo, string lagao (₹3–5 per piece)
  • Woven Label (inside tshirt): Min order 100 pcs @ ₹8–15/piece — Ahmedabad mein milte hain
  • Total packaging cost per order: ₹25–40

Operations — Roz ka Kaam

Order Processing Flow

1

Order Notification

Amazon/Flipkart app se push notification aayega. Immediately check karo.

2

Packing (2–4 hours)

T-shirt fold karo → woven label check karo → poly bag mein pack karo → thank you card daalo → seal karo → shipping label print karo

3

Dispatch (Same Day)

Amazon Easy Ship: pickup schedule karo same day mein. Same-day dispatch = better seller metrics

4

Tracking Update

Automatically hota hai Easy Ship mein. Customer ko tracking SMS milta hai.

Returns Management

⚠️ T-shirt mein Returns Zyada Aate Hain — Kya Karna Hai?
  • Size chart clearly mention karo — most returns size issues se hoti hain
  • Fabric description accurate rakho — "thin" T-shirt ko "premium" mat bolo
  • Amazon return rate 10–15% normal hai apparel mein — price mein factor in karo
  • Returned items inspect karo — resellable hai to re-pack karo, nahi to side pe rakh lo
  • Fake returns report karo Amazon pe — evidence (photos/video) se support case bana sakte ho

Inventory Management (Simple System)

📊 Free Tool: Google Sheets se karo shuruat
  • Columns: SKU, Size, Color, Purchase Price, Quantity In, Quantity Sold, Stock Left
  • Daily update karo — 5 minute ka kaam
  • Jab stock = 5 pieces bachein → reorder karo
  • Later mein free tools try karo: Zoho Inventory (free tier), Vyapar App
